"I can't remember if I took my pill — and I'm afraid to guess."

The alarm goes off, I snooze it, and five hours later I'm staring at the bottle wondering. So I skip it. Then I feel guilty all day.

✦ How we helped

Gentle reminders with one-tap logging. Missed a dose? We say "Not yet logged" — never "missed." Refill alerts before you run out.

🛡️ No medical advice — just a digital memory aid

"I had all the symptoms in my head — and forgot every one."

I had a list in my notes app. Of course I forgot to open it during the appointment. Another three months of no answers.

✦ How we helped

One-tap "Pre-visit Summary" compiles your logged symptoms, medications, and questions into a single document. Hand it to your doctor.

🛡️ We don't suggest what to say — you decide what matters

✦ How we helped

Plain-language translation of common medical terms, so you can read your results without a dictionary.

🛡️ We translate jargon — always ask your doctor for the full picture

"I needed to prove my symptoms weren't just in my head."

✦ How we helped

The Pregnancy Companion tracks BP, swelling, and headaches — generating objective, date-stamped trend reports you can print for your OB.

🛡️ You hand it over. Your doctor makes the call. We just connect the dots.

What we do. What we never do.

What We Do
  • ✓ Remind you to take your prescribed medication
  • ✓ Help you log symptoms, meals, and vitals
  • ✓ Generate trend reports from your own data
  • ✓ Translate medical jargon into plain language
  • ✓ Prepare a visit summary you can share
  • ✓ Support tracking for all bodies, all conditions

What We Never Do
  • ✕ Prescribe or change your medication
